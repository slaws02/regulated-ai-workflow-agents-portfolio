SECTION E1 — Specialist Referrals
HMO plans require primary care referral for specialist visits.
PPO and EPO plans do not require referrals.

SECTION E2 — Prior Authorization
Prior authorization is required for:
- MRI and CT imaging
- Inpatient admissions
- Specialty medications
- Outpatient surgery

SECTION E3 — Preventive Care
In-network preventive services are covered at 100%.
Examples include screening colonoscopy and annual wellness exam.

SECTION E4 — Deductible vs Out-of-Pocket Maximum
Deductible is the amount paid before plan cost sharing begins.
Out-of-pocket maximum is the yearly cap on member cost sharing.

SECTION E5 — Out-of-Network Care
Out-of-network services are covered at reduced benefit levels.
Balance billing may apply.

SECTION E6 — Pharmacy Tiers
Tier 1: Generics — lowest cost share
Tier 2: Preferred brands
Tier 3: Non-preferred brands
Tier 4: Specialty drugs — subject to prior authorization

SECTION E7 — Telehealth
Telehealth visits are covered at the same level as primary care visits when in-network.

SECTION E8 — Appeals
Members may appeal denied claims within 180 days.
